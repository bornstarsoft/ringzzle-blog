Harry Krueger, the visionary behind the acclaimed PS5 exclusive Returnal, has embarked on an exciting new journey by founding Cosmic Division, a game studio that aims to carve out its own identity in the gaming landscape. With nearly 15 years of experience at Housemarque, Krueger is ready to leverage his expertise to create innovative gaming experiences that resonate emotionally with players.

Cosmic Division's first project promises to be a single-player IP for PC and console, focusing on a gameplay-first approach and a narrative that leaves a lasting impact. This shift towards fresh themes and ideas reflects Krueger's commitment to pushing the boundaries of game design while honoring the timeless values that define great gaming.

As Krueger steps into his role as founder and CEO, he emphasizes the importance of collaboration and seeks exceptional talent to join him on this cosmic adventure. What new heights will Cosmic Division reach in the gaming universe? Only time will tell, but the excitement is palpable as we await their first creation.
