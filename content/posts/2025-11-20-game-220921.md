Tiger Woods has once again captured the spotlight, this time in a playful new commercial for the PGA Tour 2K on Nintendo Switch. The ad features the legendary golfer alongside his caddie, showcasing a humorous interaction that highlights the game's immersive experience. As Woods discusses the conditions of a par-3 hole, his caddie humorously interjects, setting the stage for a lighthearted moment that resonates with both golf fans and gamers alike.

The trailer not only provides a sneak peek into the game but also serves as a reminder of Woods' enduring popularity, even as he recovers from injuries that have kept him off the PGA Tour this season. With the PGA Tour 2K25 now available for wishlist, fans are eager to engage with the game that brings the thrill of golf right into their living rooms. As we look forward to seeing more from Woods, both in gaming and on the greens, one has to wonder: will this commercial spark a new wave of interest in golf video games?
