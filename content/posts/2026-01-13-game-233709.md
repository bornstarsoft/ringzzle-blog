SwitchTrack, the upcoming strategy coordination puzzle game from Rootward Games, is set to redefine how players engage with puzzle mechanics. In this game, players will manage tracks to ensure the safe passage of minecarts, demanding not just quick reflexes but also deep strategic thinking. This unique blend of coordination and strategy is likely to captivate both casual gamers and hardcore puzzle enthusiasts alike.

With the demo now available on Steam, players can get a taste of the challenging levels and intricate puzzles that SwitchTrack has to offer. The game promises to deliver a fresh experience in the puzzle genre, making it a title to watch as it approaches its full release. Will you be ready to take on the challenge and master the tracks?
