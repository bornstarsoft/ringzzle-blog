In a sea of free-to-play titles on Steam, few manage to capture the attention of gamers like "12, Memory Lane." Released on December 15, 2025, this indie game has quickly garnered "Very Positive" reviews, showcasing the potential of student-developed projects. With its intriguing narrative and engaging puzzle elements, it stands out as a must-play for anyone looking for a unique gaming experience.

Set in the eerie estate of the Villemaine family, players embark on a journey to uncover the mystery behind their disappearance. Utilizing Henry Villemaine's journals, youll navigate through a beautifully crafted environment filled with secrets waiting to be discovered. Despite its short runtime of around an hour and 45 minutes, the emotional depth and storytelling make it a memorable experience that resonates long after the credits roll.

While some players have noted that the game is better suited for controllers than a mouse and keyboard, the overwhelmingly positive feedback highlights the talent of the developers at Forget-Me-Not Studio. As we look to the future, its exciting to think about what this team of students will create next. Will you add "12, Memory Lane" to your library and experience this hidden gem for yourself?
