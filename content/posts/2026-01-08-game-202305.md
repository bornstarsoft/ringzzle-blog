The launch of AiliA, a captivating mirror-based puzzle game developed by IceToad Studio LLC, is creating quite a buzz in the gaming community. Available now on Steam for $11.99, with a 20% discount for early adopters, AiliA invites players to dive into a unique 3D world where mirrors are not just reflections but gateways to interactive puzzles. This innovative gameplay mechanic allows players to manipulate their environment in ways that challenge traditional puzzle-solving approaches.

Players step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blend reality with a mirrored dimension. The game boasts over 100 puzzles, including intricate water reflections and recursive mirror structures, ensuring that each challenge feels fresh and engaging. Additionally, the collaboration bundle with Moncage offers an enticing opportunity for puzzle enthusiasts to expand their gaming experience with an extra discount.

With a free demo available on Steam, now is the perfect time to explore the intertwining worlds of AiliA. Will you uncover the hidden story of Aria and her sister Lia as you solve the mysteries reflected in the game's stunning visuals?
