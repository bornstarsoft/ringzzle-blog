If you're a fan of puzzle games but often find yourself overwhelmed by their complexity, then Botany Manor might just be the perfect fit for you. Currently available for free on the Epic Games Store, this cozy first-person puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of botanist Arabella Greene as she explores a Victorian manor to complete her botanical research book. Unlike many challenging puzzle games that can leave you feeling frustrated, Botany Manor strikes a balance between engaging gameplay and a relaxing atmosphere.

The developers have intentionally crafted puzzles that are not too difficult, making it accessible for casual gamers. Laure De May, the lead developer, emphasized that the challenge lies in the number of clues and steps involved, rather than in the complexity of the puzzles themselves. This thoughtful design allows players to enjoy the game without feeling overwhelmed, making it an excellent choice for those who appreciate a more laid-back gaming experience.

With positive reviews highlighting its appeal to cozy gamers, Botany Manor stands out as a unique offering in the puzzle genre.
