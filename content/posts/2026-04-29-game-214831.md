The mobile gaming landscape is about to get a delightful addition with the announcement of Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster, a casual puzzle game that promises to engage players with its unique mechanics and charming storyline. Set to launch on iOS and Android later this year, this game invites players to step into the webbed feet of Captain Quack, who embarks on a quest to save his flock from monstrous foes while navigating through a vibrant, perilous world.

In Quack Quack Attack, players will strategically manage a team of up to five birds from a diverse roster of fifty, each belonging to one of six distinct classes. This class system not only enhances gameplay but also encourages players to experiment with different combinations to discover the most effective strategies. With features like evolving bird stats and a card system for altering abilities, the game offers depth that appeals to both casual and hardcore gamers alike.
