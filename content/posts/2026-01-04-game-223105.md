The release of iRacing's NASCAR 25 Console game marks a significant milestone for NASCAR fans, especially those who have been eagerly awaiting a multi-platform experience since 2021. With an impressive array of base paint schemes, this game not only caters to a diverse set of drivers but also introduces the ARCA Menards Series to console gaming for the first time. This expansion is a game-changer, offering players a rich selection of designs that enhance the overall racing experience.

While some fan-favorite paint schemes and sponsors are notably absent, such as Monster Energy and certain alcoholic beverage brands, the inclusion of iconic names like Red Bull signifies a nostalgic return to form for NASCAR gaming. This is particularly exciting as it marks Red Bull's first appearance in a NASCAR game since 2011, reminding us of the vibrant history and evolution of the sport.
