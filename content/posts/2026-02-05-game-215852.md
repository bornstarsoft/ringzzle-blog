Exciting news for gamers! Release dates for the Nintendo Switch 2 versions of both "Indiana Jones and The Great Circle" and "Fallout 4 Anniversary Edition" have been leaked just ahead of todays Nintendo Direct. According to the reliable insider billbil-kun, fans can expect the physical edition of "Indiana Jones" to hit shelves on May 12, 2026, while "Fallout 4" is set for an April 28 release.

While the digital release dates remain somewhat ambiguous, theres speculation that "Fallout 4" could even surprise players with a shadow drop today. This Anniversary Edition bundles the classic RPG with all its official add-ons and a plethora of user-generated content, making it a must-have for fans of the franchise. Meanwhile, "Indiana Jones and The Great Circle" has already garnered positive reviews and a significant player base since its initial release.

As we await the official announcements, the anticipation for these beloved titles on the Nintendo Switch 2 continues to grow. Will these games live up to the hype? Only time will tell!
