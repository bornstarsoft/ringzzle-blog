The 43rd annual Golden Joystick Awards showcased the best in gaming, with Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 dominating the event by winning all seven awards it was nominated for. This year's ceremony, held in London, saw over 21 million votes cast across 22 categories, highlighting the power of fan engagement in the gaming industry.

With standout titles like Ghost of Yotei and Hollow Knight: Silksong also receiving accolades, the awards not only celebrate creativity and innovation but also reflect the evolving landscape of gaming. As fans continue to shape the industry through their votes, it raises the question: what will the future of gaming look like as player preferences drive development?
