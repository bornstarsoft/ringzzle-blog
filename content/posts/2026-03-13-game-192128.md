The nominations for the BAFTA Games Awards 2026 have revealed a surprising twist, with Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 leading the charge with an impressive 12 nominations. This includes a nod for the coveted Best Game award, showcasing its critical acclaim and popularity among players and critics alike.

While Clair Obscur stands out, other notable titles like Dispatch and Ghost of Yotei also received significant recognition, with nine and eight nominations respectively. Interestingly, despite garnering seven nominations, Death Stranding 2: On the Beach failed to secure a spot in the Best Game category, highlighting the competitive nature of this year's awards.

As we look forward to the awards ceremony, it raises the question: will Clair Obscur maintain its momentum and take home the top prize, or will another title surprise us all? The anticipation is palpable as the gaming community awaits the results.
