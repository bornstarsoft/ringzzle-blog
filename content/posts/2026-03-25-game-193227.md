In an exciting revelation for gamers, the main title coming to the PlayStation Plus Essential tier for April has been leaked. According to the reliable insider billbil-kun, players can look forward to the action-RPG Lords of the Fallen, a reboot of the 2014 classic, which has garnered attention for its engaging gameplay and impressive graphics since its release in October 2023.

Set over a millennium after the original, Lords of the Fallen invites players to step into the shoes of a Dark Crusader on a quest to defeat the demon God Adyr. With a solid reception reflected in its Metacritic scores and a rapid sales milestone of one million copies within just ten days, this title is sure to be a highlight for subscribers of PlayStation Plus.

The game will be available for claim from April 7 to May 5, alongside other titles yet to be announced. As always, once claimed, these games remain accessible for play as long as the subscription is active. Are you excited to dive into the dark fantasy world of Lords of the Fallen this April?
