Moon Active has just launched Jelly Busters: Puzzle Game, and its a vibrant addition to the world of mobile gaming. This arcade-style puzzler invites players to dive into a colorful skatepark where elastic jellies skate, shoot colors, and smash through cube-filled walls. The unique gameplay mechanic of uncovering hidden images while breaking cubes adds a refreshing twist to traditional puzzle games, making each shot feel rewarding.

As players progress, they encounter increasingly challenging levels filled with obstacles and unlockable boosters that enhance gameplay. The soothing ASMR-style feedback combined with the arcade tension creates an engaging experience, whether youre a casual player or a competitive gamer aiming for the top of the global leaderboards. With Jelly Busters now available on the Google Play Store, its time to grab your skateboard and join the jelly crew in this delightful puzzle adventure!

What strategies will you employ to conquer the challenges in Jelly Busters? The games blend of relaxation and competition promises to keep players hooked for hours.
