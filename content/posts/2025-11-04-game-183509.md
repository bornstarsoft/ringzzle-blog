As the gaming community eagerly anticipates the results of the 43rd annual Golden Joystick Awards, two standout titles from the Nintendo Switch 2 library have made a significant impact. Both *Mario Kart World* and *Donkey Kong Bananza* have been nominated for the prestigious 'Console Game of the Year' award, showcasing the strength of Nintendo's latest console and its exclusive offerings.

The nominations highlight not only the competitive nature of this year's gaming landscape but also the innovative gameplay and design that Nintendo continues to deliver. With *Mario Kart World* vying for 'Best Multiplayer' and *Donkey Kong Bananza* recognized for 'Best Audio Design', it's clear that these games are resonating with players and critics alike. As the voting period opens to the public, fans are encouraged to support their favorites before the winners are announced on November 20.

With the Switch 2 already making waves in the gaming industry, one can't help but wonder what other surprises Nintendo has in store for us in the coming months. Will these nominations translate into wins? Only time will tell!
