Apple Arcade has just added two remarkable indie titles that are sure to captivate gamers: Dredge and Unpacking. Both games have been expertly optimized for mobile devices, offering unique experiences that showcase the creativity and innovation of indie developers.

Dredge+ combines fishing with elements of horror, creating a chilling atmosphere as players navigate the waters by day and face Lovecraftian monsters by night. This complete edition includes all DLC content, enhancing the gameplay with deeper narratives and challenges. On the other hand, Unpacking+ transforms the simple act of unpacking into a heartfelt puzzle game, allowing players to arrange items in a home while uncovering a touching story. Its success is reflected in its accolades, including recognition for Cultural Impact at the 2023 App Store Awards.
