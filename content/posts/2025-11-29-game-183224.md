Get ready for a whimsical adventure as the beloved Trouble Witches franchise makes its grand return! ININ, in collaboration with Studio Siesta and Rocket Engine, is set to release Trouble Witches FINAL! Episode 01 Daughters of Amalgam on December 15 for Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 4. This latest installment promises to elevate the Cute 'Em Up genre with vibrant visuals, unique gameplay mechanics, and a roster of 12 adorable witches, each with their own magical abilities.

Fans of the series can also look forward to a Physical Special Edition, available for pre-order now, which includes a physical copy of the game, an original soundtrack, a full-color artbook, and a digital download code for exclusive DLC. With its bullet-hell gameplay and charming anime-inspired aesthetics, Trouble Witches FINAL! is poised to captivate both casual gamers and hardcore enthusiasts alike. Are you ready to dive into this enchanting world of magic and mayhem?
