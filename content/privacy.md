Ringzzle is designed as a lightweight browser game.

Ringzzle does not require login, email, or a personal profile. The game stores local play stats in your browser, such as high score, today best, games played, last score, and optional sound preference.

If you submit a leaderboard score, Ringzzle stores the nickname you enter, score details, an anonymous browser player id, client version, and moderation fields needed to operate the leaderboard. The public leaderboard does not show your browser player id.

Ringzzle does not add ads, analytics, in-app purchases, or email capture in the current web game.
