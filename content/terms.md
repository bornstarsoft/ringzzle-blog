Ringzzle is provided as a free browser puzzle game.

Scores submitted to the anonymous leaderboard may be removed or rejected if they appear abusive, automated, impossible, or inappropriate. Nicknames should be suitable for a public leaderboard.

Browser-based score submission cannot be perfectly cheat-proof, so the leaderboard uses practical validation and moderation. Do not rely on leaderboard placement as an official contest, prize, or ranking.

Ringzzle may change rules, balance, presentation, or leaderboard behavior as the web game improves.
